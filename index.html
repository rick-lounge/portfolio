About Me

Hello! I'm Ricky — a front-end web developer and designer based in BC. I recently graduated from BCIT where I built real-world projects blending code and creativity. I focus on creating responsive, clean, and user-friendly websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and WordPress.

I've worked on WooCommerce sites, customized themes, and explored the e-commerce side of things too. Outside of coding, I come from a creative background — I'm into photography, video editing, and apprenticing in tattooing. That mix really helps me bring a strong visual eye and personal touch to whatever I build.

This site is my space to mix all the things I love — design, development, visuals, and some cat energy 🐾. Thanks for stopping by!
